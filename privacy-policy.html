NR Kitchen & Bedrooms is a home interior, kitchen, bedroom, wardrobe, and fitted furniture planning guide app. The app provides design inspiration, wardrobe builder tools, material guides, care tips, project checklists, and general guidance for fitted interiors.

This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

The current version of NR Kitchen & Bedrooms does not collect personal information from users.

The app does not collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location
  • Photos or camera data
  • Contacts
  • SMS or call logs
  • Payment information
  • Account login information
  • Advertising ID
  • Analytics or tracking data

2. Local Data Stored on Device

The app may store some information locally on your device only, such as:

  • Checklist progress
  • Project planning choices
  • Wardrobe builder selections
  • Notes or saved preferences inside the app

This information is stored only on your device and is not sent to NR KITCHEN AND BEDROOMS LTD or any third party.

You can clear local app data from your device settings, or from the app if a clear data option is available.

3. How We Use Information

Because the app does not collect personal data, we do not use personal information for advertising, tracking, profiling, marketing, or analytics.

Local data is used only to help the app remember your checklist progress and project planning choices.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

The current version of the app does not send user data to external servers.

5. Permissions

The app is designed to avoid unnecessary permissions.

The current version does not require:

  • Location permission
  • Camera permission
  • Microphone permission
  • Contacts permission
  • SMS permission
  • Call log permission
  • Storage/media permission
  • Payment permission

6. Third-Party Services

The current version of the app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or user tracking tools.

If future versions include services such as advertising, analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, contact forms, or external APIs, this Privacy Policy will be updated before release.

7. GDPR and User Rights

If you are located in the United Kingdom, European Union, or European Economic Area, you may have privacy rights under applicable data protection laws.

Because this app does not collect or store personal data on company servers, there is no remote account or cloud profile to access, correct, or delete.

Any checklist or planning data is stored locally on your own device. You can delete it by clearing the app data from your device settings or by using the local data deletion option if available in the app.

8. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for a general audience interested in home interiors, kitchens, bedrooms, wardrobes, and fitted furniture.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Professional Disclaimer

The app provides general design, planning, care, and maintenance guidance only.

It does not provide certified architectural, structural, electrical, gas, plumbing, tax, legal, or financial advice. For professional installation, repair, or technical decisions, users should contact a qualified professional.
